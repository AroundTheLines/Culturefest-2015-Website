What is CultureFest?

CultureFest is an annual Turner Fenton event that brings students of different backgrounds together using a diverse range of music, dance, art, food, clothing and spirit! It's a celebration of diversity, culture, and multiculturalism, historically culminating into a festival and 2.5-hour show presented each year in April. CultureFest involves hundreds of students and staff each year as performers, volunteers and organizers! Don't miss out on your chance to be part of the excitement!
